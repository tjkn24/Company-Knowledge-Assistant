Annual Leave
------------
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar
year. Leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Unused leave of up to 10
days may be carried over to the following year.

Sick Leave
----------
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical
certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.

Parental Leave
--------------
Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave.
Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks of fully paid parental leave.
Leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

Remote Work Policy
------------------
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level sign-off and a quarterly review.
All remote workers must be available during core hours (10am-3pm local time).

Health Benefits
---------------
The company covers 80% of the employee's health insurance premium and 50% of
dependent premiums. Open enrollment is held each November.
